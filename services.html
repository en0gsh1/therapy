Our Services

Sports Physiotherapy

Our sports physiotherapy program is designed to help athletes recover from injuries and improve their performance. We use advanced techniques to address sports-specific injuries and provide personalized rehabilitation plans.

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

After surgery, proper rehabilitation is crucial for optimal recovery. Our post-surgery rehabilitation programs are tailored to your specific needs, helping you regain strength, mobility, and function as quickly and safely as possible.

Chronic Pain Management

Living with chronic pain can be debilitating. Our chronic pain management program focuses on reducing pain, improving function, and enhancing quality of life through a combination of manual therapy, exercise, and education.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Our neurological rehabilitation program is designed for individuals with conditions affecting the nervous system, such as stroke, multiple sclerosis, or Parkinson's disease. We focus on improving mobility, balance, and independence.

Our Treatment Methods

Manual Therapy

Therapeutic Exercise

Electrotherapy

Using electrical stimulation to reduce pain and promote healing.

Hydrotherapy

Water-based exercises for rehabilitation and pain management.
