Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a typical physiotherapy session?

Our standard physiotherapy sessions are 45-60 minutes long. The first appointment may be slightly longer (up to 75 minutes) as it includes a comprehensive assessment of your condition.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Please wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area requiring treatment. For lower limb issues, shorts are recommended. For upper body issues, tank tops or t-shirts are suitable.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you don't need a doctor's referral to book an appointment with us. However, if you have a referral or any medical reports, please bring them to your appointment as they can be helpful for your treatment plan.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your condition, its severity, and how your body responds to treatment. After your initial assessment, your physiotherapist will provide a recommended treatment plan with an estimated number of sessions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and most major insurance plans. Payment is due at the time of service. Please check with your insurance provider regarding coverage for physiotherapy services.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee of 50% of the appointment cost. We understand that emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.
